From farm to kitchen, our carefully selected range of oils is crafted to deliver purity, nutrition, and rich flavor in every drop. At Ayush Vedica, we believe that a healthy body begins with what we cook with—and it all starts with nourishing, natural ingredients.

Our oils are minimally processed to retain their essential nutrients and authentic taste, supporting both well-being and culinary delight. Whether it's for daily cooking or special dishes, Ayush Vedica oils offer a balanced blend of health and flavor, making every meal wholesome and satisfying.

Because when health meets taste, it’s not just food—it’s nourishment that truly satisfies.

Kachi Ghani

Experience the bold aroma and rich, pungent taste of pure Kachi Ghani mustard oil. Ayush Vedica Sarso Tel brings authentic mustard flavour to your pickles and curries, enhancing every dish with a traditional touch.

Naturally extracted and carefully processed, our mustard oil offers a long shelf life while retaining its freshness and nutritional value./p>

First Pressed ( Pheli Dhaar)

Unleash the pure essence of mustard with Ayush Vedica First Pressed (Pheli Dhaar) Mustard Oil. Extracted exclusively from the finest mustard seeds using the traditional Wooden Kolhu method, this 100% first-press oil captures the rich aroma and bold flavour that only the earliest extract can offer.

Every drop delivers unmatched purity and authenticity, bringing depth and distinction to your culinary creations.

Yellow Mustard ( Peeli Sarso)

Ayush Vedica Yellow Mustard Oil (Peeli Sarso) is extracted from premium-quality yellow mustard seeds using the traditional Wooden Kolhu method. Known for its lighter pungency, golden hue, and smooth flavour, this oil is ideal for everyday cooking that calls for a milder, balanced taste.

Cold-pressed and 100% first-pressed, Ayush Vedica Peeli Sarso Oil retains its natural nutrients and aroma, making it a healthier and more flavourful choice for your kitchen.
