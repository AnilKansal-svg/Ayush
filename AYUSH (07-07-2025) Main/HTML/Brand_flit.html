Buzz off, bugs — we’ve got tech.
And it smells way better than you!

Flit is a company dedicated to creating safe, effective insect repellents made from natural ingredients. With a focus on eco-friendly formulations, Flit offers protection against mosquitoes, flies, and other pests without the use of harsh chemicals.

Their products are trusted by families, outdoor enthusiasts, and travelers for providing long-lasting defense while being gentle on skin and the environment.

Categories

Aerosol

Aerospray insect repellents are convenient, easy-to-use sprays designed to keep insects like mosquitoes, flies, and gnats at bay. Delivered in a fine mist, these repellents provide quick, even coverage on skin, clothes, or in the air, forming a protective barrier against bug bites. Often formulated with natural oils or scientifically proven ingredients, aerosprays are ideal for outdoor activities, travel, or daily protection—offering hassle-free defense with just a press of a button.

Gel

Insect repellent floor gel is a specially formulated gel designed to be applied to floors and corners to repel crawling insects like ants, cockroaches, and spiders. Unlike sprays, this gel stays in place longer, slowly releasing repellent ingredients that keep bugs away from your living space. It’s ideal for homes, kitchens, and entry points where insects are likely to enter. Easy to use and often made with natural oils or low-toxicity compounds, it provides long-lasting protection without strong odors or harmful residues.

Chalk

Insect repellent chalk, often called insecticide chalk or ant chalk, is a simple and convenient way to control crawling insects like ants and cockroaches. It looks like regular chalk but contains insect-killing chemicals. When you draw a line across entry points, windows, or along walls, it acts as a barrier—when insects cross it, they absorb the insecticide and are repelled or killed. It’s easy to apply, leaves minimal mess, and is especially useful in homes, kitchens, and storage areas. However, it should be kept away from children and pets, as it may contain toxic substances.

Glue Pad

Insect repellent glue pads, also known as insect trap pads, are flat surfaces coated with a strong, sticky adhesive designed to trap crawling insects like cockroaches, ants, and spiders. These pads are chemical-free and work by luring insects—often with scents or food-based attractants—and holding them once they step onto the surface. They are safe to use in homes, kitchens, and near food storage areas since they don’t release fumes or toxins. Easy to place and dispose of, glue pads offer a clean, non-toxic way to monitor and reduce insect activity.

Cube

Insect repellent cubes are solid, compact blocks designed to release insect-repelling fragrances or active ingredients over time. Often made with natural oils like camphor, citronella, or neem, these cubes are commonly placed in closets, bathrooms, shoe racks, or storage areas to keep away mosquitoes, moths, cockroaches, and other pests. They work by gradually emitting a scent that insects find unpleasant, creating a protective zone without the need for sprays or direct contact. Convenient, long-lasting, and low-maintenance, insect repellent cubes are a mess-free solution for everyday pest control.

Vaporiser

Insect repellent vaporisers are electric devices that disperse insect-repelling liquid or solid formulations into the air in the form of vapor. Commonly used to repel mosquitoes indoors, these devices plug into a wall socket and slowly release active ingredients like prallethrin or natural oils such as citronella. They provide continuous, long-lasting protection—especially during the night—by creating an invisible barrier that keeps insects away. Easy to use, low-odor, and effective for enclosed spaces, vaporisers are a popular choice for homes, bedrooms, and offices.

Roll-on

Insect repellent roll-on is a convenient, skin-safe product designed to provide targeted protection against mosquito and insect bites. Packaged like a deodorant stick or small bottle with a rollerball applicator, it allows easy, mess-free application directly on exposed areas like arms, neck, and ankles. Often formulated with natural oils like citronella, eucalyptus, or synthetic repellents like DEET, roll-ons are especially popular for kids and outdoor use due to their portability, precision, and long-lasting effect without spraying.

Fast Card

Insect repellent fast cards, also known as mosquito repellent cards or vapor release cards, are thin sheets infused with fast-acting insecticidal ingredients. When exposed to air or lightly activated (by tearing or placing in open areas), they quickly release vapors that kill or repel mosquitoes and other flying insects. These cards are ideal for small enclosed spaces like rooms, kitchens, or near windows. Lightweight, portable, and easy to use, fast cards provide instant protection and are especially useful during power outages or in areas without access to electric vaporisers or sprays.

Coils

Insect repellent coils are spiral-shaped, slow-burning products made from insecticidal ingredients mixed into a paste, usually containing natural extracts like pyrethrum or synthetic chemicals. When lit, the coil smolders and releases smoke that repels or kills mosquitoes and other flying insects. They are commonly used outdoors or in well-ventilated indoor areas to provide hours of protection against bites. Coils are a popular, affordable choice in many countries, especially in tropical regions, though they should be used with proper ventilation due to the smoke produced.

Incense Sticks

Insect repellent incense sticks combine the calming aroma of traditional incense with natural insect-repelling ingredients like citronella, neem, or eucalyptus oils. When burned, these sticks release fragrant smoke that helps keep mosquitoes and other flying insects away while creating a pleasant atmosphere. They’re often used indoors or outdoors during gatherings, evenings, or meditation, offering a dual benefit of fragrance and protection without harsh chemicals. Plus, they add a soothing touch to any space while helping reduce bug bites.
