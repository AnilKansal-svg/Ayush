More than clean—it's Fewa clean. Every wash, a fresh start.

Fewa is a company dedicated to creating high-quality dishwashing liquids and laundry detergents that combine powerful cleaning with care for your hands and fabrics.

Using effective, eco-friendly ingredients, Fewa products cut through grease, remove tough stains, and leave a fresh, lasting fragrance. Designed for homes and businesses alike, the brand focuses on hygiene, sustainability, and customer satisfaction—making everyday cleaning simple, safe, and effective.

Categories

Fabric Care

Our fabric care detergents are specially formulated to clean deeply while protecting the softness, color, and life of your clothes.

Whether it's everyday wear or delicate fabrics, our gentle yet powerful formula removes tough stains without harsh chemicals. Infused with a long-lasting fragrance, each wash leaves your laundry fresh, vibrant, and beautifully cared for—because your clothes deserve more than just clean.

Kitchen Care

Our dishwashing care products are designed to deliver sparkling clean dishes while being gentle on your hands.

With powerful grease-cutting action and a refreshing scent, they easily remove tough food residues and stains. Enriched with skin-friendly ingredients, our formulas ensure a safe, effective clean every time—leaving your dishes spotless and your hands soft. Perfect for everyday use in every kitchen.
