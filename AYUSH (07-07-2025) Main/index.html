Oils

Discover the purity and flavour of Ayush Vedica’s mustard oil range—Kachi Ghani, First Pressed (Pheli Dhaar), and Yellow Mustard (Peeli Sarso)—crafted using traditional cold-press methods to retain natural pungency, nutrients, and aroma. While Kachi Ghani offers a bold, robust flavour ideal for pickles and curries, First Wave delivers the purest first-press extract, and Yellow Mustard provides a lighter taste for everyday cooking. All variants offer high oxidative stability, ensuring long-lasting freshness and quality.
